About GlucoSense Patient Portal

GlucoSense is a patient portal designed to help users monitor diabetes risk, maintain health records, and access personalized health recommendations in one secure platform.

Welcome

The GlucoSense Patient Portal is designed to help patients monitor their diabetes screening records in a simple, secure, and user-friendly environment. Patients can access their prediction history, manage their profile, and download reports whenever required.

Medical Disclaimer

The prediction provided by this portal is intended for educational and screening purposes only. It should not be considered a medical diagnosis. Please consult a qualified healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
